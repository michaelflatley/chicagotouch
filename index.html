Swedish massage

It is also called European massage. It features the principles of continuous touch and transitional strokes, including:

  • Gliding strokes - Aka Effleurage
  • Compression and Kneading - Aka petrissage
  • Rocking/Shaking - Relaxes joint tissue, supports range of motion
  • Stretching - Best example: hip stretches to loosen the lower back

Deep tissue massage

Deep tissue massage, or structural bodywork includes a variety of pressure levels including sustained contact with minimal pressure.

Deep tissue massage has more emphasis on the pliability of the fascia which is the connective tissue that surrounds muscles and organs.

If the touch modality is attempting to release fascia that has become too thick, then it’s considered deep tissue bodywork. At Chicago Touch, Marco has completed a 150-hour program in Structural Integration.

Prenatal massage

Pregnancy has symptoms and natural discomfort. All aspects of healthy lifestyle take on additional significance during pregnancy. Minimizing stress becomes a higher priority.

We avoid prone massage after the first trimester and we only do side-lying massage during the third trimester.
